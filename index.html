Garbler

Web application

Garbage collection is not just a matter of the environment and urban image; It is a matter that in the face of social inequality and the mismanagement of waste by from the authorities can affect public health. In addition, an inefficiency in the collection translates into an increase in expenses.

Still in progress

Bad Waste management costs society and the region's economy 5 to 10 times plus what it would cost you to implement proper management. Thousands of Juarez complain of bad smells, accumulation of garbage and much pollution in the streets due to mismanagement.

So we pretend to create a software that monitors in real time the different garbage collection trucks and shows the user their location. Makes it aware of the route the truck takes for its pickup prediction. In this way, lost time, garbage on sidewalks is solved and the activity that the collectors carry out is monitored to supervise and optimize the traffic with a reading of time en route and thus improve the service.
